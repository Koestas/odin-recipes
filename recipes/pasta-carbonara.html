Creamy Pasta Carbonara

Description:

Pasta Carbonara, a classic Italian dish known for its creamy and savory flavor.

This recipe combines pasta, crispy bacon, and a rich sauce made from eggs, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of cream. Black pepper adds a subtle kick, balancing the dish's richness.

It's a simple yet indulgent meal, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy comfort food craving.

Ingredients:

  • ½ pound bacon, cut into small pieces
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • ¼ cup heavy cream, at room temperature
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • ¼ cup chopped parsley
  • ground black pepper to taste
